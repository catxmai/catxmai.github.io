I am a senior studying Mathematics and Computer science at Clark University. I'm applying for PhD programs in Computer science for Fall 2022 entry.

I am working with professor Michael Satz on Markov chain Monte Carlo, specifically in building models through Bayesian data analysis. This will be my senior thesis. [repo] (not too much yet)

Summer 2021, I worked with Dr. Jeff Huang (Brown HCI) on Automotion, using phone's motion replay and finger pressure to predict emotion and attention levels.
Previously, I worked on Social media analysis, specifically through video sharing with Dr. Shuo Niu. We look at YouTube-based social media campaigns and use human evaluation data to investigate participation patterns. These patterns help us understand how video-sharing platforms could support mental health and event collaboration.
