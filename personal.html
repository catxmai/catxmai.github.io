Personal

  • Go/Weiqi

    Go is a strategy board game and rose to mainstream population around 2016 - when DeepMind's AlphaGo beat Lee Sedol. Go is my biggest hobby and can always make me giddy to talk about! I play Go competitively at regional, sometimes national tournaments. My player card. I managed to win the US Open 6k division in 2018! I since have improved a lot and the playing field is much more difficult now. In summer 2020, I helped organizing the US very first e-Go Congress and co-directed the US Open. We had around 1000 attendees :) Now I'm serving on American Go Association's Diversity and Inclusion Committee, coordinating regional and national outreach efforts to underrepresented groups. NEW! I'm honored to be co-hosting our second e-Go Congress this year 2021!
