  • Go/Weiqi

    Go is my biggest hobby and can always make me so excited to talk about. I play Go competitively at regional, sometimes national, tournaments. My player card. I managed to win the US Open 6k division in 2018! But it sounds more impressive than it actually is. I've improved since and the playing field is much more difficult now.

    In summer 2020, I helped organizing the US very first e-Go Congress and co-directed the US Open. We had around 1000 attendees :)
