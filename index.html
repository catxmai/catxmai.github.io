    Hi!
    I am an undergraduate studying Mathematics and Computer science at Clark University. I'm applying for PhD programs in Human-Computer Interaction for Fall 2022 entry.


My main research has been Human-Computer Interaction with a focus in Social media analysis and social media sensemaking through video sharing with Dr. Shuo Niu. I think the best way to a man’s heart is through his YouTube homepage. We look at YouTube-based social media campaigns such as #TeamTrees and use human evaluation data to investigate participation patterns that emerge across these campaigns. These patterns help us understand how video-sharing platforms could support mental health and event collaboration.

At NGSX Learning Lab @ Clark, I had been working with Dr. Sarah Michaels on improving professional development for K-12 educators who are working with ESL learners in their Biology classes. My role was to lead the classroom discourse analysis and productive talk moves research.
